Pricing

SC Gardening provide an amazing pricing for clients of all budgets. SC Gardening will do the most as possible according to your budget. Here is what we charge.

Backyard layout design $80

You’ll get a blueprint of your backyard and some visual design ideas for you to get an idea of what you might want.

Front yard layout design $60

You’ll get a blueprint of your front yard and some visual design ideas for you to get an idea of what you might want.

Planting (starting at $500)

You’ll get your get yard landscape. This includes removal of trees, bush, weeds, and other things. SC Gardening will then redesign your yard according to the way you want.

Potting $100/pot (not including some supplies)

SC Gardening will help you pick out the pots you want and the plants and flowers you would like in it. SC Gardening charge $100/pot decorated. Clients will be paying for each pot and the plants.

Garden club $25/month

This is a club where any one can join. Come have fun with SC Gardening for monthly prizes and discounts. A gathering will be held at the end of each month. This helps fellow gardener share what they’ve learned with others.

Maintenance $75/month

After each job SC Gardening completes, clients will have a chance to opt in a monthly maintenance. SC Gardening will come and check up on your plants to make sure they are still the way you like it. SC Gardening will pull any weeds and replace any damaged plants. SC Gardening may will also replace any plants that’s out of session.
