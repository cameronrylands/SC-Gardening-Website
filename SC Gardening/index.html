Welcome to SC Gardening

SC Gardening is Known for its professional garden services. It is a relatively new business, started in the summer of 2020. It all started when the Rylands family decided to redesign their backyard. Filling a boring and blank backyard with beautiful flowers and plants. Every time guests visited the family; they always would be amazed at how nice the backyard looked. Eventually friends and family suggested that they should offer to design other people’s yard. Soon it more and more people wanted their yard redesigned too.

SC Gardening understands that gardening can be hard as it is difficult to know what design you want and what plants is for the right session. That’s why SC Gardening loves to help people with this process. Whether you’re just looking to have a few new planters in your front yard, looking to redesign your whole backyard, or simply want some advice about plants, SC Gardening has got you covered.

SC Gardening prides themselves on professional work, fare pricing, and attention to details. SC Gardening is an appointment-based business, offering their full time and resources to one client at a time. This guarantees their client the most professional work. Whether you have a small budget or an unlimited budget, SC Gardening will have something for everyone. Working with one client at a time reduces error and confusion. This allows SC Gardening to finish a job within 1-2 weeks depending on the job.
