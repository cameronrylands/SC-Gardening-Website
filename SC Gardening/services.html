SC Gardening

Types of Services

SC Gardening offer many types of services and members club to everyone. These services include but not limited to:

  1. Backyard layout – Walk though client’s backyard and map out a diagram to get an estimate on pricing and time take to complete.
  2. Front yard layout - Walk though client’s Front yard and map out a diagram to get an estimate on pricing and time take to complete.
  3. Planting – This service includes digging up unwanted or dead plants. After clearing the desired spot for planting, new trees or flowers will replace the spot. This process takes 1-2 weeks depending on the job.
  4. Potting – Talk with the client to see which pots and plants is best for them. Clients will determine how many pots they want and where they would like to put them.
  5. Buying Supplies – SC Gardening will handle the buying and delivery of all pots and plants and other supplies as needed. Clients only pay when the work is done and satisfied with the fish result.
  6. Garden Club – This club is open to everyone of all ages. In this interactive club you’ll learn all the different plants this beautiful world has to offer. This club will also tech you how to pick the right plant, when to water them, and what to look for when buying them.
  7. Monthly Maintenance – Check up on client’s flowerpot to make sure they’re still live and healthy. If plants are dead SC Gardening will replace it as no cost to the client.
